RealIn
Every card is fictional. The feelings are not.


💡 The Philosophy
LinkedIn has become a grand stage for performative professionalism. We have collectively curated a lexicon of "thrilled", "humbled", and "honoured", creating a glossy veneer that often masks the gritty reality of modern work life.

RealIn is a satirical creative experiment designed to peel back this filter. It provides a sanctuary for the stories we aren't "supposed" to tell: the "restructure" that was actually a culling, the promotion that paid in prestige rather than currency, and the deafening silence of the long-term job search.

This isn't just a parody of an interface; it's a cathartic reflection of the corporate theatre we all participate in. It’s a reminder that beneath the polished updates, our shared struggles are what make us human.

⚖️ Disclaimer
All characters and narratives within RealIn are entirely fictional. Any resemblance to your current manager, your HR department, or that one awkward "life update" from your ex-colleague is entirely intentional. No employers were harmed in the making of this project (though their buzzwords certainly were).

===

💡 專案理念
LinkedIn 已經演變成一場「表演式專業」的大型舞台。我們共同構築了一套由「深感榮幸」、「由衷感激」組成的詞彙庫，在完美的社交濾鏡下，掩蓋了現代職場最真實、也最粗糙的面貌。

RealIn 是一個帶有諷刺意味的創意實驗，旨在撕開這層濾鏡。它為那些「不被允許」說出的故事提供了一個避難所：那些被包裝成組織重組的裁員、那些只給名分不給加薪的升職，以及長期待業中那種震耳欲聾的沉默。

這不僅僅是對介面的惡搞，更是對我們共同參與的「職場劇院」的一種宣洩與反思。它提醒著我們：在那些光鮮亮麗的動態背後，真實的掙扎才讓我們顯得有血有肉。

⚖️ 免責聲明
RealIn 內的所有角色與敘事均為虛構。若有任何情節與您的現任主管、人資部門、或是某位前同事那篇尷尬的「近況更新」雷同，那完全是故意的。本專案製作過程中沒有任何雇主受到傷害（但他們的職場術語肯定受傷不輕）。
